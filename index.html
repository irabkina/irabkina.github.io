About Me

Hello, and welcome to my page! I am a recent graduate of [Scripps College](www.scrippscollege.edu) (BA, Neuroscience 2014), a soon-to-be graduate of [Loyola University Chicago](www.luc.edu) (MS, Computer Science 2015), and a soon-to-be student of [Northwestern University](www.northwestern.edu) (PhD, Computer Science). As a researcher, my interest is at the intersection of how our knowledge about the brain can inform computation (i.e. Artificial Intelligence) and how computation can inform our knowledge about the brain (i.e. Neural/Cognitive Modeling). As an academic, my interest is in making computer science education more approachable, especially to girls and women. As a Californian transplanted to Chiberia, my interest is in finding warmth in the winter, dry air (and a microbrew) in the summer, and some mountains in between.
